What we recover

Every type of hidden asset
we specialise in

🏡

Dormant Estate Property

Inherited land, houses, and buildings that were never formally transferred or that sat unclaimed in probate. We trace ownership chains back decades to establish your entitlement.

🏦

Unclaimed Financial Assets

Bank accounts, building society accounts, premium bonds, shares, and insurance policies belonging to deceased relatives or opened and forgotten years ago.

📜

Intestate Estate Claims

When someone dies without a will, their estate may escheat to the state. We identify overlooked beneficiaries and pursue claims against state unclaimed property offices and probate courts on your behalf.

🌍

International Property Recovery

Property, land, and assets held abroad in other jurisdictions. Our network of international partners enables us to pursue claims across multiple legal systems simultaneously.
